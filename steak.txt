Ingredients:
 - 1 steak
 - 2 pinch salt
 - 2 pinch pepper
 - 1/2 Tbsp butter

Directions:
 1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F. 
 
 2. Pat dry, and apply a pinch of salt and pepper to each side of steak.

 3. Heat an oiled cast-iron skillet on high heat.

 4. Sear each side of steak, including edges, using tongs to hold the steak on its edge.

 5. When eery side is seared, place butter in skillet and move the steak on top of it.

 6. Put steak in oven for ~5 minutes, measuring internal temperature to 135-140 degF.

 7. Let steak rest for 5 minutes before serving
